What is this?

This is a tool for Eve Online aimed at being able to quickly price check items in bulk.

What's new?

2019-01-24 - moved to latest go-evepraisal

How are prices calculated?

Prices calculated from recent market data from Eve Online's ESI API. Items with low (or no) market volume use CCP's pricing estimates.

What does a purple row mean?

Purple rows are items that Skypraisal had a hard time finding an accurate and current price for. This is usually caused by low (or no) market volume for that item.

What does logging in do?

Currently, logging in gives your own personal history of appraisals. It also removes the banner advertisemenet that is placed on the bottom of each page. I plan to add the ability to remove appraisals and more customization/settings that will be persisted with your user.

Caveats/Known Issues

  • There's no reliable way to get prices of blueprint copies (BPCs). Due to this, Skypraisal doesn't even try to guess a price for them.
  • Non-english formats don't always work properly.

Is there an API?

Yes! A very simple one, but it will probably get you what you want. Click here to see the API Documentation.
